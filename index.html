About

The Eye
Behind the Lens

I'm Connor Chong, a Las Vegas photographer specializing in pet photography, family photos, and portraits. My focus is creating natural, high-quality images that capture genuine personality and connection.

Every session is approached with care and attention to detail, ensuring you receive photos you'll be proud to share and display. Professional photo editing is available for just

per photo, and I also offer printed and framed photographs so your favorite memories can become lasting artwork.

Whether you're celebrating your family, your pet, or yourself, I'm committed to delivering images that stand out and last a lifetime.
