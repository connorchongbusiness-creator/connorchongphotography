About

The Eye
Behind the Lens

I'm Connor Chong — a photographer based in Las Vegas, Nevada, with a passion for capturing moments that linger long after the shutter clicks. My work spans landscapes, portraits, street photography, and live events.

Every frame is an opportunity to freeze something fleeting — the quality of light at dusk, an unguarded expression, the geometry hidden in everyday spaces. I bring the same level of intention to every project, no matter the scale.

Available for commercial, editorial, and personal commissions. Let's create something worth keeping.
